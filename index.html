Our Story

An Unforgettable Culinary Philosophy

"Cooking is not just technical skills, it is an expression of soul, nature, and art."

Established in 2010 in the heart of Paris, Aurum represents the pinnacle of premium dining. We select only the finest ingredients sourced directly from local, sustainable farms, transforming each component into a visual and sensory masterpiece. Every dish tells a story of tradition, innovation, and absolute perfection.

Culinary Visionary

Alessandro Moretti

Executive Chef & Founder

With over 20 years of experience leading Michelin-starred kitchens across Paris, Milan, and Tokyo, Chef Alessandro Moretti brings a worldly, innovative approach to classic gastronomy. His philosophy centers around highlighting natural product flavors through creative textures and absolute technical perfection.

Frequently Asked Questions

Read about our booking guidelines, dress codes, and dining policies

We enforce a smart elegant dress code. Jackets are recommended for gentlemen. Athletic wear, T-shirts, shorts, sneakers, or beach attire are not permitted in the main dining hall.
Reservations can be canceled or rescheduled up to 48 hours prior without penalty. Cancellations within 48 hours are subject to a fee of $50 per guest.
Yes, our chefs can customize the menus for severe allergies or dietary preferences (vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free). Please note your requirements in detail when making a reservation.
Complimentary valet parking is available for all guests at our main entrance on Rue de la Gastronomie. Secured self-parking is also available nearby.
